Section 3 Fun Notes

Manitoba Act of 1870

In 1870 the Manitoba Act was signed after Louis Riel's Provisional government showed the government a bill of rights. This established the 5th Province of Canada called Manitoba.

PEI joins Confederation

Prince Edward Island joins the Confederation and becomes the 7th Province. They got railroad funding, 6MPs, annual payments and land back from absentee British landowners.

Creation of the Northwest Mounted Police

In 1873 The Northwest Mounted Police was established to enforce law in the Northwest Territories. They would force whiskey traders out, deliver mail, and fight fires.

In 1884 The Northwest Resistance took place. Louis Riel and Gabriel Dumont were involved. They battled at Batoche for 3 days before surrendering. Louis Riel was hanged the next year.
